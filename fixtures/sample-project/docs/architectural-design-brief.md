# Architectural Design Brief

> **Sample document — testing only.** This file exists to give Castor
> users realistic prose to query in Ask and to propose changes against
> in Modify. Codes, dimensions, and material values are written in EU
> (Eurocode / Spanish CTE) format and **may be technically wrong** —
> spotting and flagging discrepancies is part of the demo, not a bug.
> Do not use any value here as a basis for real design.

## 1. Scope

This brief covers the architectural intent for a four-storey mixed-use
building comprising ground-floor retail, three storeys of residential
apartments above, and a partial basement for plant and storage. It is
issued at Design Development for coordination with the structural,
MEP, and FF&E packages. Drawings, schedules, and specifications cited
here are referenced by document number; nothing in this brief shall be
taken as superseding a stamped drawing.

## 2. General requirements

- All internal heights shall be **2.70 m clear** in residential units
  and **3.20 m clear** in retail units, measured from FFL to underside
  of slab.
- Accessible routes shall comply with **CTE DB-SUA 9** with a clear
  width of not less than **1.20 m** through corridors and **0.85 m**
  through doors.
- Acoustic separation between dwellings shall achieve a minimum
  airborne **DnT,A ≥ 50 dB** and impact **L'nT,w ≤ 60 dB**.
- Fire compartmentation shall provide **EI 60** between dwellings and
  **EI 90** between dwellings and the basement.

## 5. Doors and windows

- **Apartment entrance doors:** solid core, 45 mm, fire-rated EI 60-Sm
  with self-closer and intumescent seals. Lock to BS 8621.
- **Internal apartment doors:** hollow-core, 40 mm, painted MDF
  facings.
- **Bathroom doors:** hollow-core with privacy lock, undercut 10 mm
  for ventilation.
- **External windows (residential):** triple-glazed argon-filled, U ≤
  1.1 W/m²K, openable casement on at least one leaf per room.
- **Retail shopfronts:** structural glazing, single full-height span,
  manual recessed entrance door.

## 6. FF&E (Design Development scope)

The architectural package coordinates the loose furniture and built-in
joinery shown on the room datasheets (drawings ARCH-FF&E-101 to 305).
Loose furniture is selected by the interior team but subject to the
architectural acoustic and fire constraints in §2.

Per typical dwelling unit:

- Living / Dining: 1× three-seat sofa, 1× armchair, 1× side table, 1×
  dining table for four, 4× dining chairs, 1× sideboard.
- Master bedroom: 1× double bed (1.50 × 2.00 m), 2× bedside tables,
  1× wardrobe (built-in, 2.40 m wide, full-height).
- Secondary bedroom: 1× single bed (0.90 × 2.00 m), 1× desk, 1× chair,
  1× wardrobe (built-in, 1.80 m wide).
- Kitchen: built-in U-shape with 600 mm dishwasher, 600 mm oven,
  induction hob, integrated extractor.
- Bathroom: ceramic WC, 1.20 × 0.80 m walk-in shower with glass
  screen, 800 mm vanity unit.

All built-in joinery shall be flush-fronted MDF, painted to match
adjacent wall finish unless noted otherwise on the room datasheets.
