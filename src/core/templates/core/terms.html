CastorIQ is a private testing environment. Access is invite-only. By signing in you agree to use the service in good faith and accept the limitations below.

No warranty

The beta is provided as-is, without any warranty. Things will occasionally break. Don't run business-critical workflows on it. Keep your own backups of any IFC file you upload — although CastorIQ versions changes via Git, the service may be unavailable when you need it most.

Acceptable use

  • Don't upload content you don't have the right to share.
  • Don't try to extract data from other users' projects.
  • Don't use CastorIQ as a relay to attack other systems or services.
  • Don't try to bypass the daily token cap or the master kill-switch.
  • Don't share your account credentials.

Token allowance

Each account has a daily allowance of cloud LLM tokens, visible inside the app. The cap exists to keep the beta affordable; if you need it raised for a real evaluation, ask the operator.

LLM output

Answers from any LLM — local or cloud — can be wrong. Modify proposals are routed through a tiered review pipeline, and the highest-risk tier requires human review before any change touches your IFC. You are responsible for verifying outputs before relying on them in a real project.

Termination

Either side can end the relationship at any time. You can permanently delete your account from Settings → Danger zone. We may revoke access if these terms are broken.

Changes

These terms may be updated as the beta evolves. Material changes will be communicated by email to active users.
