A few things to know about the beta

  • It's a private testing environment. We're a small team running this on a single VPS. Things will occasionally break.
  • When you ask Castor a question or propose a modification, the request is processed by cloud LLM providers (Anthropic, Groq) when they are active in our admin config. Don't upload anything you wouldn't share with those providers under their standard policies.
  • You have a daily token cap (visible inside the app). It resets at midnight UTC. Email us if you need it raised.
  • You'll see a sample IFC project pre-loaded on first login — use it to explore Ask and Modify before uploading your own files.

If anything looks wrong, email support@castoriq.io — a real human reads it.
