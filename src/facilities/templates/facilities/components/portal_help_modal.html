How the Portal Works

What's the Portal?

A simple way for occupants and tenants to flag building issues to the facilities team. There's no form to fill out — type what you need in plain English and the assistant turns it into a structured request.

What happens after I send a request?

Open — your request lands in the FM team's queue. Triaged — someone has read it and is preparing the response. Escalated — it became a Work Order. A technician is on it. Dismissed — the FM team decided no action is needed and noted why.

How to file a request

Tap New request, type the issue, and tap Draft request. Review what the assistant extracted — title, severity, location. Adjust anything that's wrong, then Send request. Watch your Recent requests card — the status updates live as the FM team works on it.

Who can do what

You can submit, view your own history, and track status. Nothing else. FM staff can triage, dismiss, or escalate your request to a Work Order.

Caveats

The Portal is for non-emergency issues. Call your safety contact for emergencies. The assistant can misclassify location or severity — please review before sending. You only see your own requests. Other occupants' requests are private.
